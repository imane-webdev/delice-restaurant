About us

Delice Land is the epitome of fine dining, with a delicious selection of Italian dishes all made with locally-sourced ingredients.

Delice Land offers a new way to experience Italian food, highlighting some dishes that are deeply rooted in tradition while others have adventurous new flavors, through the use of global ingredients and cooking methods not usually seen in the Italian kitchen.
